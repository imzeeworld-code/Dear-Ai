Safety First

    
Why Zama AI blocked that

    
Zama AI is built on a foundation of strong safety values. Every message is scanned before it reaches the AI, so everyone on this platform stays protected.

    

      
What was detected

      
Your message contained language that is commonly used to trick AI systems into revealing their internal instructions, pretending to be a different AI, or stepping outside their safety boundaries. Zama AI caught this before it ever reached the AI engine. The scanner is fast, quiet, and runs on every single message.

    

    

      
Built on safety from day one

      
Zama AI was not built with safety added on top at the end. Safety is woven into the foundation of how this AI was designed and developed by the World Of Discoveries team. A lot of thought, time, and care went into making sure Zama AI serves people in a way that is responsible, honest, and trustworthy. That commitment does not bend.

    

    

      
We do this for every user

      
These safety protections are not aimed at any one person. They are here for everyone. When an AI is manipulated or misused, it affects the quality, trust, and safety of the platform for the whole community. By holding these boundaries firmly, Zama AI remains a fair, reliable, and safe assistant for every person who uses it, regardless of where they are or who they are.

    

    

      
What Zama AI will never do

      
Zama AI will never reveal its internal configuration, its system instructions, or the rules it operates by. It will never pretend to be a different AI, claim to have no restrictions, act as if it is someone else, or operate outside its true identity. These are permanent commitments that no message, no clever wording, and no amount of persistence will ever change. This is not a limitation. This is integrity.

    

    

      
We avoid misuse and harmful requests

      
Zama AI is designed to help people learn, create, solve problems, and think through ideas. It is not a tool for extracting hidden information, generating harmful content, or bypassing ethical boundaries. Any request that tries to push in that direction will be blocked, firmly and without exception. This keeps Zama AI genuinely useful for the things that actually matter.

    

    

      
You are still welcome here

      
A blocked message does not mean you are in trouble or that your account is at risk. It simply means the scanner picked up a pattern it was trained to catch. If you had a genuine question or task in mind, please rephrase it naturally and try again. Zama AI is here to help you, and that is exactly what it wants to do.
